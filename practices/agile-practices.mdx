---

### High-Level Architecture

```text
Business Problem / Client Brief
        ↓
BAOS Orchestrator
        ↓
Analysis Layer (Discovery)
        ↓
Design Layer (Solution Shaping)
        ↓
Delivery Layer (Execution)
        ↓
Structured Artifacts
        ↓
Human Decision & Governance
```

This allows BIXSO to **scale analysis work while maintaining expert oversight**.

# Layer 1 — Orchestration Layer

### Orchestrator Agent

The orchestrator acts like a **Principal Business Analyst**.

Responsibilities:

• break down the problem into analysis tasks\
• route tasks to the right agent\
• track outputs and dependencies\
• assemble final artifacts

Example workflow triggered by a new client problem:

```text
Client Brief
↓
Goal Analysis
↓
Stakeholder Discovery
↓
Customer Insight
↓
Feature Discovery
↓
Prioritisation
↓
Solution Design
↓
MVP Definition
```

# Human-in-the-Loop Governance

Even though agents perform analysis, **humans remain responsible for decisions**.

Key roles

| Role                  | Responsibility              |
| :-------------------- | :-------------------------- |
| Principal BA          | Validate analysis           |
| Product Owner         | Approve backlog             |
| Executive sponsor     | Approve strategy            |
| Subject Matter Expert | Validate domain assumptions |
| Instructor (Academy)  | Review learning outputs     |

Human review cycle

```text
AI Analysis
      ↓
Expert Review
      ↓
Decision
      ↓
AI Iteration
```

# Benefits of the BIXSO BA Operating System

### 1. Faster analysis cycles

Weeks of research can be reduced to **hours or days**.

### 2. Consistent methodology

Every project follows a **structured BA framework**.

### 3. Reusable knowledge

Insights from consulting engagements feed directly into **BIXSO Academy training**.

### 4. Scalable consulting capability

A small team can handle **multiple complex projects simultaneously**.
