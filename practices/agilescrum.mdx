## ns BIXSO as **three integrated businesses** that reinforce each other.

### Core Ecosystem Structure

```text
Knowledge
      ↓
BIXSO Academy
      ↓
Capability
      ↓
BIXSO Consulting
      ↓
Technology
      ↓
BIXSO Agentic Platform
```

Each component generates **value and data that strengthen the others**.

---

# 1. BIXSO Consulting (Revenue Engine)

Consulting is the **primary revenue generator** and source of real-world insights.

### Services

• Digital transformation strategy\
\
• Product discovery and innovation\
\
• Business analysis capability development\
\
• AI-assisted transformation programs

### Output

Consulting engagements produce:

- transformation roadmaps
- solution designs
- implementation plans

More importantly, they generate **knowledge assets**.

Example outputs:

```text
Industry case studies
Transformation playbooks
Best-practice frameworks
```

These insights feed the rest of the ecosystem.

# 2. BIXSO Academy (Capability Engine)

The academy transforms consulting insights into **training and professional development programs**.

### Programs

• Business Analysis foundations\
\
• Product discovery and innovation\
\
• AI-assisted analysis techniques

Students include:

- business analysts
- product managers
- digital transformation teams

The academy serves two purposes:

1. Develop future consultants and practitioners
2. Spread BIXSO methodologies across organizations

This creates **market influence and brand authority**.

• automated discovery analysis\
\
• AI-assisted product design\
\
• decision modeling and prioritization\
\
• automated generation of BA artifacts

Example outputs:

```text
Impact maps
Stakeholder analysis
```

This platform allows BIXSO to **scale consulting work without scaling headcount linearly**.
