# The BIXSO Global Expansion Model

The expansion strategy focuses on **three scaling engines**:

1️⃣ Global Certification\
2️⃣ Strategic Partnerships\
3️⃣ Digital Platform Marketplace

Together they create **international reach without relying on physical offices**.

---

# 1. Global Certification System

Certification is the **most powerful lever for international growth**.

The idea is to create a **recognized credential in AI-augmented Business Analysis**.

### Example Certifications

**BIXSO Certified AI Business Analyst (CAIBA)**

Skills covered:

• AI-assisted discovery\
• product innovation techniques\
• decision modeling\
• MVP definition

**BIXSO Certified Agentic Product Strategist**

Skills covered:

• agentic workflows\
• product discovery using AI\
• strategy modeling

---

### Certification Model

```text
Online Training
      ↓
Hands-On Case Study
      ↓
Assessment by AI + Instructor
      ↓
Professional Certification
```

Benefits

• builds global brand authority\
\
• creates a practitioner network\
\
• drives demand for the BIXSO platform.
