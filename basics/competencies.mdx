# Core Competencies

This guide outlines the essential competencies and services that Business Analysts provide in Agile environments.

## Requirements Analysis

As a business analyst, my core competencies include eliciting, documenting, and analyzing requirements. By providing this service, I can help define project scope, identify key objectives, and lay the foundation of successful project delivery.

### Key Activities
1. **Requirements Elicitation**
   - Stakeholder interviews
   - Workshops and focus groups
   - Document analysis
   - Observation and job shadowing

2. **Requirements Documentation**
   - User stories
   - Use cases
   - Business rules
   - Functional specifications

3. **Requirements Analysis**
   - Gap analysis
   - Impact assessment
   - Feasibility studies
   - Risk evaluation

### Deliverables
- Requirements documentation
- Process flows
- Data models
- Business rules catalog

## Process Improvement

As a business analyst, I can assist in identifying areas for process improvement and optimization. This can involve conducting process assessments, analyzing workflows, identifying pain points, and recommending solutions.

### Key Activities
1. **Process Assessment**
   - Current state analysis
   - Pain point identification
   - Bottleneck detection
   - Efficiency evaluation

2. **Workflow Analysis**
   - Process mapping
   - Value stream mapping
   - Time and motion studies
   - Resource utilization analysis

3. **Solution Design**
   - Process redesign
   - Automation opportunities
   - Technology integration
   - Change management planning

### Deliverables
- Process maps
- Improvement recommendations
- Implementation roadmaps
- ROI analysis

## Business Development

I can offer expertise in conducting feasibility studies, analyzing costs and benefits, and formulating a persuasive business case. This service helps articulate the value proposition of projects based on a comprehensive assessment of potential risks and returns.

### Key Activities
1. **Feasibility Studies**
   - Market analysis
   - Technical feasibility
   - Operational feasibility
   - Financial feasibility

2. **Cost-Benefit Analysis**
   - Cost estimation
   - Benefit quantification
   - ROI calculation
   - Risk assessment

3. **Business Case Development**
   - Value proposition
   - Strategic alignment
   - Implementation plan
   - Success metrics

### Deliverables
- Feasibility reports
- Business cases
- Cost-benefit analyses
- Implementation plans
