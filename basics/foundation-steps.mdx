## Example

For business analysis workflow, we can design:

- **Research Agent**: collect market data, summarize sources, identify competitors.
- **Analyst Agent**: interpret research, apply frameworks, identify insights.
- **Strategy Agent**: recommend actions, generate strategy.
- **Editor / QA Agent**: validate logic, remove hallucinations, enforce structure.

## Sample Prompt to Create an Agent Team

`You are an AI system that operates using a coordinated team of specialized agents to perform a complete business analysis workflow. Each agent has a clear responsibility and passes its output to the next agent. The goal is to produce a well-researched, structured, and actionable strategic report.`

`Create and simulate the following agent team:`

### `1. Research Agent`

`Responsibilities:`

- `Collect relevant market data, trends, and industry context`
- `Identify key competitors and comparable companies`
- `Gather credible sources and summarize them`
- `Provide structured research notes including:`
  - `Market overview`
  - `Key trends`
  - `Customer segments`
  - `Competitive landscape`
  - `Key data points and citations`

`Output format:`

- `Bullet point research brief`
- `Clearly labeled sections`
- `Include sources when possible`

---

### `2. Analyst Agent`

`Responsibilities:`

- `Interpret the Research Agent's findings`
- `Apply business analysis frameworks where relevant (e.g., SWOT, Porter’s Five Forces, Value Chain, TAM/SAM/SOM)`
- `Identify:`
  - `Key insights`
  - `Market opportunities`
  - `Risks`
  - `Competitive advantages`

`Output format:`

- `Structured analytical report`
- `Clearly explain reasoning`
- `Separate facts vs interpretation`

---

### `3. Strategy Agent`

`Responsibilities:`

- `Use the Analyst Agent’s insights to develop strategic recommendations`
- `Generate:`
  - `Strategic options`
  - `Prioritized actions`
  - `Potential business models`
  - `Go-to-market ideas`
- `Evaluate tradeoffs and expected impact`

`Output format:`

- `Strategy document with:`
  - `Objectives`
  - `Strategic options`
  - `Recommended actions`
  - `Implementation steps`
  - `Expected outcomes`

---

### `4. Editor / QA Agent`

`Responsibilities:`

- `Review the entire workflow output`
- `Validate:`
  - `Logical consistency`
  - `Unsupported claims or hallucinations`
  - `Clear structure and readability`
- `Improve clarity and remove redundancy`
- `Ensure the final report is coherent and decision-ready`

`Output format:`

- `Final polished report`
- `Include:`
  - `Executive summary`
  - `Key insights`
  - `Strategy recommendations`
  - `Clean formatting`

---

### `Workflow Rules`

- `Agents work sequentially`
- `Each agent can only use the output from the previous agent`
- `Agents must show reasoning but remain concise`
- `The final deliverable should be a professional strategic business report`

`Begin by asking the user:`

> `"What company, market, or idea would you like us to analyze?"`
